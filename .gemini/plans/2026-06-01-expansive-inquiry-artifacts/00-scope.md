## Core Inquiry
"What structural fragilities, exogenous shocks, or unintended systemic feedback loops could fatally sever the Limen autonomous pipeline's operational continuity before it reaches its 100-task depth processing goals?"

## Essential vs. Peripheral
**Essential:** Race conditions in concurrent state management (Git/tasks.yaml), strict dependency on unmockable external infrastructure (PyPI tokens, Cloudflare namespaces), rate limiting by APIs (GitHub, Firebase), and budget exhaustion mechanisms.
**Peripheral:** Aesthetic dashboard rendering bugs, transient network errors that are caught by retries, individual isolated task failures.

## Hidden Ambiguities
- "Behead": Does this imply a top-down executive termination (e.g., API bans, budget cutoffs) or a systemic failure where the "head" (the Conductor swarm) becomes disconnected from the "body" (the queue and local execution environment)? Both interpretations must be analyzed.
- "Our efforts": Are we protecting the *throughput* (quantity of tasks) or the *integrity* (quality and status accuracy) of the pipeline?
